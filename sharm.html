Welcome to Sharm El-Sheikh

Sharm El Sheikh is a popular resort town located on the Sinai Peninsula in Egypt, known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs. It is a major tourist destination, attracting visitors for its world-class diving spots and luxurious resorts. The city also offers a variety of activities, including water sports, desert safaris, and cultural experiences.

Where to go in Sharm El-Sheikh

khalig nema

Naama Bay is a vibrant and popular area in Sharm El Sheikh, renowned for its beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters. It's a lively hub for tourists, offering numerous restaurants, shops, and nightlife options. The bay is also a prime location for water activities such as snorkeling, diving, and beach relaxation.

Hollywood Sharm El Sheikh

Hollywood Sharm El Sheikh is a lively shopping and entertainment complex inspired by classic Hollywood glamour. It offers a wide range of shops, restaurants, cafes, and attractions, making it a popular spot for both locals and tourists. Visitors can enjoy the vibrant atmosphere, stunning architecture, and entertainment options, including live performances and themed events.

The Monument to the Victims of the Plane Crash

The Monument to the Victims of the Plane Crash in Sharm El Sheikh commemorates the tragic 2015 crash of a Russian passenger plane. Located near the airport, it honors the 224 lives lost in the disaster, serving as a place of remembrance and respect. The memorial is a poignant reminder of the tragedy and the lives affected by it.

ghibli-raceway

Ghibli Raceway in Sharm El Sheikh is an exciting go-kart racing track that offers thrilling experiences for both beginners and seasoned drivers. The track features high-quality go-karts and a challenging course, making it a popular destination for adrenaline seekers. It's a great spot for families, friends, and groups looking to enjoy some fast-paced fun in a safe environment.

SAFARI

Safari in Sharm El Sheikh offers an exciting adventure through the vast desert landscapes surrounding the city. Visitors can explore the Sinai Peninsula by jeep, camel, or quad bike, discovering stunning natural beauty and remote Bedouin villages. The experience often includes activities like stargazing, traditional meals, and the chance to learn about local culture and history.

Al-Mustafa mosque

Al-Mustafa Mosque is a beautiful mosque located in Sharm El Sheikh, known for its impressive architectural design and serene atmosphere. The mosque features intricate decorations, grand domes, and towering minarets, making it a prominent landmark in the city. It serves as a place of worship and is also a popular spot for tourists seeking to appreciate its beauty and peaceful surroundings.

Dancing Fountain at Soho Square

The Dancing Fountain at Soho Square in Sharm El Sheikh is a stunning water display that combines music, light, and motion. It features synchronized water jets that move to rhythmic tunes, creating a mesmerizing visual experience for visitors. The fountain is a popular attraction, especially in the evening, offering a vibrant atmosphere at one of the city's most lively entertainment hubs.
