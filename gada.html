welcome to Hurghada

Hurghada is a popular resort city located on Egypt's Red Sea coast. Known for its stunning beaches, vibrant coral reefs, and crystal-clear waters, it attracts tourists for diving, snorkeling, and other water sports. The city also offers a variety of resorts, restaurants, and lively nightlife, making it a top destination for both relaxation and adventure.

Where to go in Hurghada

TIG MARINA

Tig Marina in Hurghada is a luxurious waterfront development, offering a mix of shops, restaurants, and cafes along the Red Sea. It’s a popular spot for both tourists and locals, providing stunning views and a relaxing atmosphere. The marina also hosts a range of boats and yachts, making it a prime location for boating enthusiasts.

Giftun ISLAND

Giftun Island, located just off the coast of Hurghada, is a beautiful, unspoiled paradise known for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant coral reefs. It's a popular destination for snorkeling, diving, and relaxing on pristine beaches. The island offers a peaceful escape, making it a must-visit for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

mahmya giftun island

Mahmya on Giftun Island is a beautiful, serene beach destination near Hurghada, known for its white sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters. It's a popular spot for snorkeling, offering vibrant coral reefs and abundant marine life. The island provides a peaceful retreat with sunbeds, restaurants, and a relaxed atmosphere.

jungle aqua park

Jungle Aqua Park in Hurghada is one of the largest waterparks in Egypt, offering a wide variety of slides and attractions for all ages. With over 35 water rides, pools, and a lazy river, it’s a fun destination for families and thrill-seekers. The park's jungle-themed design adds an exciting and adventurous vibe to the experience.

steigenberger pure lifestyle

Steigenberger Pure Lifestyle in Hurghada is a luxury, adults-only resort offering an exclusive and sophisticated experience. With modern design, private pools, and top-tier dining options, it caters to guests seeking relaxation and luxury. The resort's serene atmosphere, combined with world-class amenities, makes it a perfect getaway for couples and adults.

ALF-LEILA-WLEILA

Alf Leila Wa Leila is a popular cultural and entertainment complex in Hurghada, offering visitors a glimpse into Egypt’s rich history and traditions. The resort features a spectacular nightly show, showcasing traditional music, dance, and folklore. With its grand architecture and vibrant atmosphere, it’s a must-visit for those wanting to experience Egyptian culture in a unique setting.

Almina Mousque

Al Mina Mosque in Hurghada is a stunning piece of Islamic architecture, known for its grand design and intricate details. Located near the city’s marina, it serves as a central place of worship and a peaceful spot for reflection. The mosque’s striking minarets and beautiful courtyard make it a notable landmark in the city.
